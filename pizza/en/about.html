About us

Italian pizzeria for the whole family. Everyone knows how reverently and respectfully Italians are to his family. That is why we have created a small family restaurant, where everyone will be comfortable and where everyone will find a favorite delicious! The menu is quite diverse, we are constantly updating and improving it, depending on tastes and preferences of our guests. But most importantly, without which we can not exist, which makes our hearts beat faster - of course, Pizza!

Those who have tasted our pizza say that it is one of the best in the city. We have more than 20 types of pizza, for absolutely every taste.

We use Italian flour of the highest quality from the manufacturer Molino di Pordenone, a special pizza variety - "00". Their flour Sole di Napoli Rosso is created especially for the test of long growth with addition of the minimum amount of yeast. This is what it provides Neapolitan pizza has a lush crispy crust, and all its fans - lightness after each slice.

Fast. Deliciously. For you.
